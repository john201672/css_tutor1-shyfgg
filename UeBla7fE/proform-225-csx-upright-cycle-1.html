DESCRIPTION

The ProForm® 225 CSX features iFit® technology so you can reach your goals with Google Maps™ routes, workouts designed by certified personal trainers, and stat tracking. The built-in tablet holder keeps all your favorite fitness apps and entertainment close by. And with 20 digital resistance levels, you can customize every workout to meet your needs. Plus, an adjustable oversized seat with added cushioning provides a more comfortable ride.

MAIN FEATURES

iFit® Bluetooth® Smart Enabled
Take your fitness goals all over the world with our revolutionary interactive training program, iFit® technology◊. Train with programs designed by a certified personal trainer, map your route with Google Maps™, track your progress automatically, compete with friends, and customize your workout to match your goals.

Integrated Tablet Holder
Make fitness time multi-tasking time. This device shelf keeps your tablet secure and conveniently close so you have access to all your training tools and entertainment must-haves.

Inertia-Enhanced Flywheel
Experience a smooth, steady pedal stroke with the flywheel made to perform.

Freewheel Clutch
The 225 CSX bike was built to imitate the ultimate outdoor cycling experience. With the freewheel clutch, the circular motion is smooth and natural so you can maintain your form and pedaling technique just like you would on your outdoor cycle.

Oversized Cushioned Seat
Don’t just work out—enjoy a comfortable ride. This oversized seat has added cushioning and easily adjusts so you find the perfect fit.

Ergonomic Pedals with Adjustable Straps
Designed to keep your feet in place, these pedals are built with adjustable toe straps to increase stability.

SMR™ Silent Magnetic Resistance
This smooth, quiet resistance system allows you to adjust your workout intensity without interrupting your workout.

Transport Wheels
Easily move the bike around the room with the built-in transport wheels.

Leveling Feet
Keep your bike stable on an uneven surface with these adjustable leveling feet.

Round Watts LED Display
Have you ever wondered if your workout is making a difference? With our new Watts LED Display you can watch your power output right on console with bright LED lights. Based on our power algorithm of watts per kilogram, you can tell instantly if you are in the Endurance, Tempo, or Peak zone. Endurance is a great area for a slow and steady workout. The Tempo zone places you in your ideal heart rate based on your weight category, and Peak is where you reach your cardio max. And if you are focusing on interval training, press the Work button to kick it in high gear, then press Recover to slow it down.

iPod® Compatible Audio*
Rock your workout to your favorite music. Simply plug your iPod* into the Intermix Acoustics 2.0 Sound System and listen to incredible sound through two 2-inch speakers.

20 Workout Apps
Stay motivated with 20 built-in workouts. These programs take the guesswork out of your workout by automatically adjusting the resistance of the exercise bike for increased intensity. The only thing you have to worry about is pressing start.

20 Digital Resistance Levels
For higher workout intensity, quickly adjust your resistance level with the console controls.

CoolAire™ Workout Fan
Keep cool while you sweat with this built-in fan that features two speed settings. Quick console controls allow you to adjust the speed at any time.

EKG™ Grip Pulse
Stay in your target heart rate zone for goal-specific workouts. Built-in heart rate sensors help you monitor your heart rate throughout your workout.

AC Adapter Included
Plug in this wall adapter to power your console electrically if you prefer not to use batteries.

275 Lb. Weight Capacity
This bike accommodates up to 275 pounds.

Manufactures Warranty
The ProForm® 225 CSX Upright Bike is protected with a Lifetime Frame Warranty and a 1-Year Parts & Labor Warranty.
